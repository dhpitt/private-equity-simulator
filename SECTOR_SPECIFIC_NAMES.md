# Sector-Specific Company Names

## Overview

All companies, regardless of size, now have a chance of having names that indicate their sector. This makes it easier to identify what industry a company operates in just from its name.

## The Change

### Before
- **Local-style names** (e.g., "Tony's Liquor Store"): 100% sector-specific
- **Corporate-style names** (e.g., "Global Industries Inc"): 0% sector-specific (completely generic)

**Problem**: Large companies (>$5M revenue) had 80% chance of corporate names, meaning 80% of large companies had completely generic names that didn't hint at their sector.

### After
- **Local-style names**: Still 100% sector-specific
- **Corporate-style names**: Now 50% sector-specific
